How to use Volleyball Rotation Tracker
  1. Input the player numbers from the lineup directly into the player lists on the leftmost and rightmost sides of this app.
  2. Click the On Court! buttons for either team to view their lineup visually on the diagram/court.
  3. Tick the Serving box to designate the first serving team.
  4. Click +1 on the corresponding team's side when they get a point. The rotation and score will be tracked for you.
  5. If you have made a mistake, click the Undo button to undo the last action. Previous actions may be undone as well.
